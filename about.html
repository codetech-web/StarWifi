Our Story

Delivering High-Speed Internet Across Your Area

On behalf of Sree Murugan Vision, our cable TV company has been serving Puliyampatti and its surrounding areas for the past 25 years, earning the trust of over 2,000 customers. Building on that, we launched our internet service in 2018 in partnership with BSNL, utilizing fiber cable technology, and subsequently introduced our private network service under the name Star Net in 2019. Currently, over 850 customers are benefiting from high-quality internet services through both BSNL and Star Net. In addition, we are now providing our customers with advanced features like IPTV and OTT services.
